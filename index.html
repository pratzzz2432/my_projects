A temperature converter app is a mobile application designed to convert temperatures between different units of measurement, such as Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. Users can input a temperature in one unit, and the app will quickly provide the equivalent temperature in the desired unit. These apps are useful for people who need to work with temperature data in various formats, such as travelers, scientists, and engineers.
